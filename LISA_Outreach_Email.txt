## Brief Introduction to Our Theory

We have developed a "Fixed 4D Topology-Dynamic Spectral Dimension Multiple Twisting Fractal Clifford Algebra Unified Field Theory" that unifies gravity, quantum mechanics, and the standard model gauge forces through geometric principles.

The key innovation is the prediction of **six gravitational wave polarization modes** (versus two in general relativity):

1. **Tensor modes (2)**: h₊, hₓ — standard GR
2. **Vector modes (2)**: h_x, h_y — predicted amplitude ~τ₀/2 ≈ 5×10⁻⁷  
3. **Scalar modes (2)**: h_b, h_l — predicted amplitude ~τ₀²/3 ≈ 3×10⁻¹³

where τ₀ = 10⁻⁶ is the single free parameter determined by experimental constraints.

The additional polarization modes carry signatures that could be distinguished using LISA's triangular configuration and network analysis techniques.

### Secondary Predictions

1. **Propagation speed differences**: Vector/scalar modes travel at slightly different speeds (v = c(1 - ατ₀²)), leading to arrival time delays of ~10⁻⁵ seconds over cosmological distances

2. **Modified waveforms**: The complete six-polarization waveforms differ subtly from standard GR templates

3. **Stochastic background**: The primordial gravitational wave background has modified spectral properties

## Falsifiability

We emphasize that our theory makes concrete, falsifiable predictions:

- **If LISA detects only 2 polarization modes**: Core prediction falsified, theory requires fundamental revision
- **If vector polarization amplitude differs from τ₀/2**: τ₀ parameter constrained differently
- **If no dispersion in arrival times**: Speed difference prediction incorrect
