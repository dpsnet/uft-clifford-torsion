### 4.2 量子涨落模型

```python
d_s(T) = d_s_smooth(T) + δd(T)

其中:
- d_s_smooth = 4 + 6/(1 + exp(-2*ln(T/T_GUT)))
- δd ~ N(0, σ²), σ = 0.05 * fluct_scale(T)
- fluct_scale = 1/(1 + exp(-2*(ln(T/T_GUT) - 1)))
```

涨落幅度在T_GUT附近最大，高低温时冻结。
