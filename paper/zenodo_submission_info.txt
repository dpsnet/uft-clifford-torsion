Fixed 4D Topology-Dynamic Spectral Dimension: Multiple Twisting Fractal Clifford Algebra Unified Field Theory

**Description/Abstract**:
We present a unified field theory based on fixed 4D topological spacetime with a dual 10-dimensional internal spectral space. The framework introduces a novel concept of reciprocal internal space duality where 10-dimensional internal space and 4D spacetime are dual to each other---neither containing the other, but complementary aspects of a unified geometric structure. Energy oscillates between these dual spaces in the form of spectral flow, creating interference effects that explain observed deviations from pure geometric calculations.

Key results include: (1) derivation of U(1)×SU(2)×SU(3) gauge symmetries from multiple twisting mechanisms; (2) geometric origin of CKM matrix with 99.9% agreement using energy oscillation interference; (3) six gravitational wave polarization modes including scalar and vector components; (4) geometric interpretation of time as spectral dimension flow.

The theory makes falsifiable predictions: additional GW polarizations at 10^-5 level, characteristic CMB modifications, and energy-scale dependent CKM elements. Experimental constraints (LIGO/Virgo, Planck CMB) are satisfied with torsion field strength τ₀≈0.005, derived from first principles as the information fidelity of projection between the dual spaces.

This research was conducted through human-AI collaboration.
