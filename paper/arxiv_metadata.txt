**Abstract** (限制 1920 字符):

We present a novel mechanism for core-collapse supernova explosions within the Clifford-Torsion Unified Field Theory (CTUFT). In this framework, the stellar core undergoes a "spectral dimension collapse" where the spectral dimension of the 4D reciprocal space approaches zero as the internal space energy fraction approaches unity. The fundamental information quantum τ₀=0.005 acts as a "valve" that controls energy accumulation and release, naturally explaining the ~200-fold enhancement of explosion energy and the observed parity violation in neutrino emission. We calculate the chiral-dependent neutrino escape times: left-handed neutrinos escape in ~6.6 ms while right-handed neutrinos are suppressed by τ₀² with escape times of ~1.3 s. The theory predicts an early νₑ pulse leading by ~1-2 ms, spectral hardening of the neutrino energy spectrum, and a characteristic 6 ms delay between gravitational waves and neutrinos. For repeating fast radio bursts, CTUFT predicts P_FRB = 200 × P_spin, consistent with FRB 180916's 16.35-day periodicity. These distinctive signatures are testable by next-generation detectors including Hyper-Kamiokande, DUNE, and Einstein Telescope.
