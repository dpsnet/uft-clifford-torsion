\subsection{Dynamic Spectral Dimension}

The spectral dimension varies continuously with energy scale:

\begin{equation}
    d_s(E) = 4 + \frac{6}{1 + (E/E_c)^2}
\end{equation}

where $E_c = M_P / \tau_0 \sim 2 \times 10^{21}$ GeV is the critical energy scale.

At different energy regimes:
\begin{itemize}
    \item $E \ll E_c$: $d_s \approx 4$ (topological reciprocal space dominates)
    \item $E \sim E_c$: $d_s \approx 7$ (transition regime)
    \item $E \gg E_c$: $d_s \approx 10$ (spectral internal space fully manifest)
\end{itemize}

This flow represents the same physical system appearing with different effective dimensionality at different energy scales—not a decomposition of 10 dimensions into 4+6, but a unified description where the dimensionality itself is dynamic.
