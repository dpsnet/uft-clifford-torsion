\section{Experimental Tests}
\label{sec:experimental}

Overview of experimental tests for CTUFT.

\subsection{Gravitational Wave Tests}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Six polarization modes (vs. two in GR)
    \item SNR $\sim$ 1000 for LISA intermediate-mass mergers
    \item Characteristic $\tau_0 \approx 0.005$ modulation
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Particle Physics Tests}

Precision measurements of fermion masses and mixings.

\subsection{Astrophysical Tests}

Dark matter, dark energy, and primordial black hole signatures.
