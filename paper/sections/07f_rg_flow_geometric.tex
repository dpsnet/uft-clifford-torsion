\section{Renormalization Group Flow}
\label{sec:rg_flow}

Geometric interpretation of renormalization group flow.

\subsection{RG Equations}

The running of couplings with energy scale:
\begin{equation}
\frac{dg_i}{dt} = \beta_i(g_1, g_2, g_3), \quad t = \ln(\mu/\mu_0)
\end{equation}

\subsection{Unification in CTUFT}

All gauge couplings unify at the Planck scale due to the geometric origin of interactions.
