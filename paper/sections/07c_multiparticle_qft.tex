\section{Multiparticle QFT}
\label{sec:multiparticle}

The multiparticle extension of CTUFT quantum field theory.

\subsection{Fock Space Construction}

For $N$ identical particles, the Hilbert space is the symmetric (bosons) or antisymmetric (fermions) tensor product:
\begin{equation}
\mathcal{H}_N = S_\pm (\mathcal{H}_1^{\otimes N})
\end{equation}

\subsection{Second Quantization}

Field operators create and annihilate particles:
\begin{align}
\hat{\psi}(x) &= \sum_k \left[ \hat{a}_k \phi_k(x) + \hat{b}^\dagger_k \phi_k^*(x) \right] \\
\hat{\psi}^\dagger(x) &= \sum_k \left[ \hat{a}^\dagger_k \phi_k^*(x) + \hat{b}_k \phi_k(x) \right]
\end{align}
