\section{Complete Fermion Mass Calculation}
\label{sec:fermion_masses}

Complete calculation of fermion masses from geometric principles.

\subsection{Mass Generation Mechanism}

Fermion masses arise from the interaction with the Higgs field:
\begin{equation}
m_f = y_f \frac{v}{\sqrt{2}}
\end{equation}

where $y_f$ is the Yukawa coupling and $v \approx 246$ GeV is the Higgs vacuum expectation value.
