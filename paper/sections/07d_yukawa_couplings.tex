\subsection{Geometric Yukawa Formula}

The Yukawa coupling between fermions and the Higgs field arises from the torsion field overlap:
\begin{equation}
y_{ij} = g_Y \int d^{10}x \, \sqrt{-g_{(10)}} \, \bar{\psi}_i(x) \psi_j(x) \phi_H(x) \tau(x)
\end{equation}

where $\tau(x)$ is the torsion field and $g_Y$ is the geometric coupling constant.
