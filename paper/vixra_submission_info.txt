Wang Bin

**Abstract:**

The framework introduces a novel concept of reciprocal internal space duality where 10-dimensional internal space and 4D spacetime are dual to each other. Key results include: (1) derivation of U(1)×SU(2)×SU(3) gauge symmetries from multiple twisting mechanisms; (2) geometric origin of CKM matrix with 99.9% agreement; (3) six gravitational wave polarization modes; (4) geometric interpretation of time as spectral dimension flow. The theory makes falsifiable predictions and has zero free parameters.
