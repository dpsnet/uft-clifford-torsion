\subsection{Key Distinctions of CTUFT}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Zero free parameters}: Unlike other approaches, CTUFT has no adjustable parameters.
    
    \item \textbf{Fixed topological dimension}: 4D spacetime is fixed, not emergent or extra.
    
    \item \textbf{Dynamic spectral dimension}: $d_s$ varies while topology remains 4D.
    
    \item \textbf{Mathematical rigor}: Complete axiomatic treatment provided in this work.
\end{enumerate}
