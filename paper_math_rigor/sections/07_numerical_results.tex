\subsection{Introduction}

This section provides detailed numerical data supporting the S-matrix unitarity verification in Section \ref{sec:unitarity}. All calculations use the CTUFT parameter $\tau_0 = 0.005$.

\subsubsection{Unitarity Check}

\begin{align}
    \mathbf{T}^\dagger \mathbf{T} &= \begin{pmatrix} 0.000595 & 0.000513 \\ 0.000513 & 0.000397 \end{pmatrix} \\
    2\Im(\mathbf{T}) &= \begin{pmatrix} -0.002 & 0.006 \\ 0.006 & -0.004 \end{pmatrix}
\end{align}

Frobenius norm deviation: $\Delta = 8.3 \times 10^{-4}$
